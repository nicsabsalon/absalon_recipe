Lasagna Recipe

This classic lasagna recipe is made with an easy meat sauce as the base. Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly! This is great for feeding a big family and freezes well, too.

Procedure:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F. Brown ground meat in a large skillet on medium heat and stir occasionally. Mix spaghetti sauce into meat mixture.
  2. Spread 1 cup of meat sauce onto the bottom of a small to medium-sized baking dish and top with lasagna noodles until the layer is full.
  3. Spread half of the cheese mixture over the noodles.
  4. Repeat meat sauce, noodles, and cheese layers once. Top with remaining noodles and meat sauce. Make sure to cover noodles with sauce. Sprinkle the remaining 1/2 cup of Eden Melt Sarap on top. Cover with foil.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes. Remove foil. Bake for 15 minutes or until noodles are tender and cheese is golden brown. Let stand for another 15 minutes before cutting.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 blocks cheese
  • 6pcs. Lasagna noodles
  • 1pc large Onion,Chopped
  • 4 cloves Garlic Cloves,Chopped
  • 1/2 1bs. Ground Beef/ Pork
  • 500g Spaghetti Sauce
  • 250g Tomato Sauce
  • Salt & Peppre to taste
  • 5tbs.Butter
  • 4 tbs.All Purpose Flour
  • 2 1/2 cups Milk
  • 1pc Egg

Spaghetti Recipe

Homemade spaghetti sauce is a crowd pleaser. Make a large batch, and freeze for future quick and easy meals.

Procedure:

  1. Add the beef and onions to a Dutch over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook, breaking up the beef into very small pieces, until the beef is browned and the onions are tender, about 6 minutes.
  3. Drain off any grease, if necessary. Stir in the garlic, oregano, and basil, and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Add tomatoes.Stir in the tomato paste until well incorporated.
  6. Add the diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and sugar.
  7. Bring to a low boil, then reduce to a simmer. Simmer the sauce for at least 30 minutes and up to 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  8. Taste the sauce and add the balsamic vinegar and more sugar, salt, and pepper, as needed

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef / pork
  • 1 medium sweet onion, finely chopped (about 3/4 cup)
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp. dried oregano
  • 2 tsp. dried basil
  • 1/4 tsp. black pepper
  • 1 (6 oz.) can tomato paste
  • 1 (16 oz.) can tomato sauce
  • 1 Tbsp. granulated sugar
  • 1 Tbsp. balsamic vinegar (optional)
  • 1 (16 oz.) package spaghetti,cooked according to package instructions and kept warm.
